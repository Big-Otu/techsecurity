About the Event

In today’s connected world, digital threats are real — and they don’t just target banks or businesses. Churches, families, and individuals are increasingly vulnerable to scams, identity theft, and online manipulation.

Safe in the Spirit is a dynamic, interactive digital safety workshop designed for the church community. Hosted by TechSecurity , it equips you with practical tools to protect your personal data, family, and ministry online — all grounded in faith and responsibility.

Join us for an engaging day of learning, fellowship, and empowerment as we explore how to live boldly and safely in the digital age.
