About the Workshop

We conducted a hands-on workshop at Assemblies of God Church to equip church members with simple but powerful ways to protect their social media accounts and phones.

Many people lose control of their Facebook, WhatsApp, Telegram, TikTok and other social media accounts to scammers and impersonators. This workshop taught practical steps everyone can start using today.

Topics We Covered

Social Media Security

  • ✓ Spotting and stopping impersonation accounts
  • ✓ Strong passwords and Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
  • ✓ What to do if your account is hacked
  • ✓ Safe privacy settings on Facebook, WhatsApp, Telegram & Tiktok

Phone Security Tips

  • ✓ Strong screen lock (PIN, fingerprint or face ID)
  • ✓ Keeping software updated
  • ✓ Safe app downloads and permissions
  • ✓ Using “Find My Device” if phone is stolen
  • ✓ Regular backups of important data
